## 📌 About

This repository is a comprehensive DSA syllabus designed specifically for **placement preparation at top product companies (FAANG, startups, service companies)**. Every topic is hand-curated and tagged by:

- **Difficulty** — Foundational / Intermediate / Advanced
- **Importance** — Must-know topics (⭐)
- **Interview frequency** — Topics that appear most often (🔁)

> **Goal:** Give you a clear, no-fluff map of everything you need to master — nothing missing, nothing unnecessary.

---

## 🗺️ Study Roadmap

Follow this order for maximum efficiency. Each stage builds on the previous one.

```
Stage 1 (Week 1-2) ──── Arrays & Strings · Sorting & Searching · Math & Bit Manipulation
Stage 2 (Week 3-4) ──── Linked Lists · Stack & Queue · Hashing
Stage 3 (Week 5-6) ──── Trees (Binary Tree, BST, Segment Tree, BIT)
Stage 4 (Week 7-8) ──── Graphs (BFS, DFS, Dijkstra, MST, Union-Find)
Stage 5 (Week 9-11) ─── Dynamic Programming (all patterns)
Stage 6 (Week 12) ───── Greedy · Backtracking · Divide & Conquer
Stage 7 (Week 13+) ──── Trie · Heap · Advanced DS · System Design for Coding
```

> For companies like Google, Meta, Amazon — **Stage 1–6 is the minimum bar.** Stage 7 differentiates senior roles and competitive positions.
